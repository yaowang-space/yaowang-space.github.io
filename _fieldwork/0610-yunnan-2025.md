## Geological Field Investigation — Sanjiang Region, Yunnan

**Date:** June 2025  
**Location:** Sanjiang (Three Parallel Rivers) region, Yunnan Province, SW China  
**Focus:** Tectonic and magmatic evolution of the Sanjiang Tethyan orogenic belt

---

### Overview

Field investigation targeting Mesozoic–Cenozoic magmatic rocks in the Sanjiang region, part of the eastern Tethyan tectonic domain. Collected samples for petrological, geochronological, and geochemical analyses.

### Key Activities

- **Rock sampling**: Collected ~50 rock specimens from granitic intrusions and volcanic sequences
- **Structural mapping**: Documented fault systems and fold geometries
- **Stratigraphic logging**: Measured and described key geological sections
- **Field photography**: Captured high-resolution images for 3D outcrop modeling

### Geological Significance

The Sanjiang region records the closure of the Paleo-Tethys Ocean and subsequent India–Eurasia collision, making it a natural laboratory for studying orogenic processes and continental crustal evolution.
