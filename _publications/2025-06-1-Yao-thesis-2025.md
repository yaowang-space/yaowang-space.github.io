Undergraduate thesis completed at China University of Geosciences (Beijing), June 2025.

*Advisor: Ze Liu*  
*Research Area: Zircon U-Pb Geochronology & Depth Profiling*

<div style="margin-top: 15px; padding: 12px; background: #f0f7ff; border-left: 4px solid #2196F3; border-radius: 4px;">
<strong>Abstract:</strong> This study quantitatively evaluates zircon depth profiling plateau ages, developing objective criteria for identifying and validating age signal plateaus in laser ablation inductively coupled plasma mass spectrometry (LA-ICP-MS) depth profiling data.
