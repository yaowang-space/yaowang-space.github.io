🏫 My first paper was featured on the **China University of Geosciences (Beijing) official news**: [自动化定量提取锆石深度剖面U-Pb定年数据新方法](https://www.cugb.edu.cn/bdkx/51036.jhtml).

---

### 📰 Summary

The article highlights our newly developed **Automated Depth Profiling Technique (ADEPT)** — a quantitative workflow for extracting U-Pb ages from zircon depth profiles, published in *Journal of Geophysical Research: Solid Earth*.

Key findings:

- **ADEPT** combines ARIMA anomaly detection, LOESS smoothing, and PELT segmentation to objectively identify age signal plateaus
- Bayesian validation: **>84%** of plateau data consistent within 1× RMSE
- Applied to **4,615 Himalayan leucogranite zircons**, revealing four major magmatic pulses at **45 ± 2, 35 ± 3, 19 ± 4, and 9 ± 2 Ma**
- Introduced **ΔAge** parameter, showing a systematic eastward decrease across the Himalaya — consistent with eastward slab tearing/delamination models
