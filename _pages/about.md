I am a Master's student at the **School of Earth Sciences and Resources, China University of Geosciences (Beijing)**. My research sits at the intersection of **petrology, geochronology, and computational geosciences**, aiming to quantitatively decipher the evolutionary history of the lithosphere.

## 🎯 Research Vision

My long-term goal is to advance **quantitative earth and planetary science** through:

- **Method Development** — Establishing new analytical techniques for high-resolution geochemical measurements (e.g., [Automated Depth Profiling Technique, ADEPT](https://doi.org/10.1029/2025JB033324))
- **Multi-scale Integration** — Bridging micro-scale mineral records with macro-scale tectonic processes
- **Predictive Modeling** — Constraining geological processes via numerical modeling, experimental petrology, and data-driven approaches
