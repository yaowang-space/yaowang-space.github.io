Research assistant (2022–present) affiliated with the following groups:

---

### 1. 🏔️ Magmatism and Tibetan Plateau Formation

Led by **Prof. Di-Cheng Zhu**, this group investigates the magmatic processes associated with the growth and evolution of the Tibetan Plateau, integrating petrology, geochemistry, geochronology, and geodynamics.

🌐 [http://www.tibetzhu.com/](http://www.tibetzhu.com/)

---

### 2. 🧠 Deep Thinking Lab

Led by **Associate Prof. Ze Liu**, the Deep Thinking Lab develops computational and data-driven approaches to geological problems — including high-throughput geochemical data processing, machine learning applications, and automated geochronology workflows.

🌐 [https://zeliu-cugb.wixsite.com/geology](https://zeliu-cugb.wixsite.com/geology)

---

### 3. 🔬 MILMA — Mineral Laser Microprobe Analysis Laboratory

The MILMA lab at CUGB provides state-of-the-art in-situ microanalytical facilities for mineral characterization, including LA-ICP-MS for trace element and isotopic analysis.

🌐 [https://bm.cugb.edu.cn/gpmr/c/2025-10-31/838037.shtml](https://bm.cugb.edu.cn/gpmr/c/2025-10-31/838037.shtml)
